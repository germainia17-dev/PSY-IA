# Companion IA - Beta Testing Guide

## What We're Testing

**Monetization & Retention Flow** — Does the soft paywall work better than a hard block?

You'll be testing:
1. **Onboarding** — 2-screen version (name + intent on one screen)
2. **Evolution tracking** — Themes, streak, mood history visible on journey
3. **Daily limit** — When you hit the limit, you see two options:
   - "Voir ton évolution" → View your progress
   - "Continuer maintenant" → Upgrade to Pro

## Key Metrics We're Tracking

When you test, these events are logged **locally on your phone** (privacy-first):
- When you hit the daily limit
- Which button you click (view evolution vs upgrade)
- If you complete a Pro purchase
- Your return days (streak building)

## How to Test

### Day 1: Experience the promise
1. **Onboarding**: Go through the 2-screen setup (name/status/intent)
2. **First chat**: Talk to the AI about what's on your mind
3. **Feel understood**: The AI should show it understands your situation
4. **Write until limit**: Keep talking until you hit the daily message limit
5. **Hit the limit screen**: You'll see the soft paywall with 2 options

### Day 2+: Build the habit
1. **Check your journey** — See your streak, mood history, recurring themes
2. **Come back daily** — The habit-building signal (if soft paywall works, you should want to return)
3. **Track themes** — Watch what topics keep appearing across sessions
4. **Optional upgrade** — If you want unlimited, click "Continuer maintenant" → Pro

## Testing Checklist

- [ ] Onboarding completes without friction
- [ ] First response feels personalized (uses your name/status/intent)
- [ ] Chat feels natural and understanding
- [ ] Daily limit is clear but not frustrating
- [ ] Evolution screen shows meaningful data (themes, streak, moods)
- [ ] Coming back tomorrow feels like a habit-builder, not a punishment
- [ ] Pro offer feels optional, not forced

## Feedback to Share

After 3-7 days of testing, tell us:
1. **Did you come back daily?** (streak building working?)
2. **Which button did you click at the limit?** (view evolution or upgrade?)
3. **Did you upgrade to Pro?** Why or why not?
4. **What felt friction-y?** (hard to use, unclear, too many screens)
5. **What felt natural?** (the understanding, the tracking, the habit)

## Important Notes

- **Privacy**: All your chat history and evolution data stays on your phone — nothing is sent to us
- **Daily limit**: 5 messages/day free, unlimited with Pro
- **Analytics**: We track which buttons you click to measure if the soft paywall works
- **Bugs**: If something crashes or doesn't work, note it + when it happened

## How to Report Issues

1. **In-app bug**: Describe what happened + screenshot
2. **Feedback**: Use the settings screen or email directly
3. **Analytics access**: We can show you your own events logged (see Settings → Analytics for testing)
